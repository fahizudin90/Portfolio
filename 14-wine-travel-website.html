Wine, a couples-focused luxury escape platform.

Overview

I redesigned Wine, a travel platform offering affordable luxury getaways for couples. The original site felt cluttered and lacked emotional pull, so I rebuilt it around simplicity, storytelling, and a clearer sense of exclusivity and romance.

Approach

The new page runs from a hero section through “Why Choose Us,” featured destinations, testimonials, and a closing CTA, aimed at couples in their late twenties to forties planning a getaway.
