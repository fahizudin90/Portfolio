GENERAL PROJECT · APR 2025

Sign-up, from wall of fields to a guided flow.

Overview

I redesigned a business platform’s sign-up flow, turning a long single-page form into a guided, multi-step flow to reduce how overwhelming it felt to fill out.

Problem

The original page grouped user details, business details, and subscription details onto one screen with no progress indication. Users would stop halfway, unsure how much was left.

Approach

I replaced it with a 3-step flow, user details, business info, then verification, each with a progress indicator, following the same stepper pattern used by Google, Stripe, and Shopify for multi-field sign-ups.

Impact

  • Tested internally with the new stepper-based flow live
  • Early feedback pointed to less frustration and more confidence completing sign-up
